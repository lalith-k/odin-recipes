COCONUT BURFI

Description

It is a common Indian dessert recipe prepared during Diwali, Holi, Raksha Bandhan & Navaratri

Burfi is a popular treat that is enjoyed as a sweet anytime snack and shared during Indian festivals. A burfi itself is simply a type of soft fudge, and can be made with nearly endless other ingredients to create a wide range of flavors.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Coconut
  • Milk
  • Ghee
  • Condensed milk
  • Sugar
  • Green Cardamom powder

Steps

  1. Grate 3/4 cup coconut and soak in milk.
  2. Heat 1 table spoon ghee in pan.
  3. Add milk soaked coconut to pan.
  4. Pour 3/4 cup sweet condensed milk.
  5. Stir and mix thoroughly.
  6. Add 1tbsp sugar and 1/4 tbsp green cardamom powder.
  7. Stir often and continue to cook the mixture.
  8. Continue to cook until you see ghee coming out from the sides.
  9. Now set it on tray. Cool and serve.
