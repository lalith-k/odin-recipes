SUSHI RICE

Description

Sushi is a very popular Japanese Dish.

Sushi rice is made of a different type of rice that has higher starch content and that is why is is more stickier than normal rice.

Sushi rice has ot be prepared before making sushi.

Ingredients

  • Sushi Rice
  • Rice Vinegar
  • White sugar
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Add the rinsed rice to a saucepan with water (use 3 parts water to 2 parts rice). Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and cook, covered, until the rice is tender and all the water has been absorbed.
  2. Combine the rice vinegar, oil, sugar, and salt in a separate saucepan. Cook over medium heat until the sugar has dissolved.
